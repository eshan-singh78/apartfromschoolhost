About Us

Apart From School (AFS)

Apart From School (AFS) is a team of passionate industry professionals on a mission to prepare school students for the real world. We believe that true learning goes beyond textbooks and exams—students need practical exposure, hands-on skills, and guidance to make confident decisions about their future.

At AFS, we organize workshops, training camps, and mentorship programs designed for Classes 7th–12th. Our focus is on building industry-level skills in areas like Cybersecurity, Game Development, Web Development, Robotics, Digital Media, Soft Skills, Leadership, and Entrepreneurship.

What makes us different is our trainer network—experienced professionals who have trained the Indian Army, Police Forces, NCC, BSF Commandos, and have also been invited by leading schools and universities across India. Backed by collaborations with E-Cell NFSU Delhi and associations with companies like Microsoft, Adobe, Airtel, and Google, we ensure students get not just training but also industry exposure.

While competitions and collaborations like our Inter-School Tech & Skills Championship are exciting opportunities for students to showcase their talent, our primary mission is to help them discover their strengths, skill up in the right direction, and prepare for the future with confidence.
